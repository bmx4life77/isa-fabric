# **Article XXV — Scale Invariance Doctrine**

25.1 All constitutional operations SHALL be invariant under scaling transformations.

25.2 For any scalar λ:

κ → λκ SHALL NOT change governance outcomes.

25.3 All curvature, thresholds, and transitions SHALL be defined using normalized ratios.

25.4 κ_ref SHALL:

* be deterministically computed
* be stored canonically
* satisfy persistence window constraints

25.5 Any operator violating homogeneity SHALL be rejected.

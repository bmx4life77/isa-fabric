# **Article XXVII — Quantum Interpretive Layer**

27.1 A probabilistic quantum layer MAY operate over embedded state space.

27.2 This layer SHALL:

* be strictly interpretive
* never modify Σ(t)
* never influence ψ₅ or SE

27.3 Quantum outputs:

* may inform analysis
* may inform dashboards
* SHALL NOT gate execution

27.4 Decoherence events SHALL:

* trigger conservative mode
* be logged to BLVDB

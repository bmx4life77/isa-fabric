# ⭐ **4. Doctrine of Curvature Spike Containment**  
*(Geometric Doctrine — Hyperbolic Divergence Control)*

**Definition:**  
This Doctrine establishes the constitutional requirement to detect, classify, and contain curvature spikes — sudden hyperbolic divergences in κ_total or Ricci scalar that threaten system stability.

**Principles:**  
1. **Spike Detection:** The system must continuously monitor curvature for divergence.  
2. **Hyperbolic Containment:** Curvature spikes must trigger conservative mode.  
3. **Rollback:** Severe spikes require rollback to the last stable curvature state.

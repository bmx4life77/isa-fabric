# ⭐ **Doctrine of Non‑Regression Enforcement**  
*(Constitutional Doctrine — Integrity Preservation)*

**Definition:**  
This Doctrine establishes the constitutional requirement that no governance action, operator, or evolution may degrade prior integrity levels or violate previously satisfied invariants.

**Principles:**  
1. **Zero Regression:** Regression delta must remain exactly zero.  
2. **Invariant Preservation:** All invariants must remain satisfied across evolution.  
3. **Rollback:** Any regression triggers rollback to the last non‑regressed state.

# ⭐ **Doctrine of Lineage Integrity**  
*(Constitutional Doctrine — Fork Safety & Ancestral Purity)*

**Definition:**  
This Doctrine establishes the constitutional requirement that lineage remain sealed, drift‑free, and consistent across all forks, mitosis events, and divergence pressures.

**Principles:**  
1. **Ancestral Purity:** All forks must inherit sealed, uncorrupted lineage.  
2. **Divergence Detection:** Any deviation from sealed lineage constitutes drift.  
3. **Revocation:** Drifted forks must be isolated, rolled back, or revoked.

**P.1 Purpose**  
The Invariarch Stratum is the **constitutional integrity organ** of ISA Fabric. It serves as a non-authoritative, non-normative guardian layer that continuously observes, evaluates, and protects the foundational invariants of the organism across all Pillars. The Invariarch Pentad forms the core of this stratum and operates as a closed integrity loop.

**P.2 The Invariarch Pentad**

The Pentad consists of five tightly coupled invariants. Each member has distinct responsibilities but contributes to a unified integrity vector \( I(t) = [ID, EI, LC, RV, BS] \) embedded in Group 4 of Σ(t).

**2.1 Invariant Density (ID)**  
Monitors overall coherence, compactness, and non-redundancy of Σ(t). Detects fragmentation, duplication, or excessive sparsity. Flags density drift and recommends controlled calibration events that may trigger Preisach wiping-out.

**2.2 Enforcement Integrity (EI)**  
Protects the correctness, boundedness, and purity of all enforcement actions (primarily Class F). Verifies that enforcement remains strictly metric-gated and prevents enforcement creep into interpretation.

**2.3 Lineage Continuity (LC)**  
Guarantees perfect replay verifiability and causal stratification from genesis. Monitors event ordering and ensures BLVDB memory remains sufficient for deterministic reconstruction.

**2.4 Reversibility (RV)**  
Ensures the organism always retains recoverable paths back to the safe basin. Monitors recovery trajectories and prevents irreversible commitments.

**2.5 Boundary Stability (BS)**  
Guards the fundamental geometric and threshold invariants (α < β, embedding immutability, envelope coherence, Separation Doctrine boundaries).

**P.3 Pentad Interaction Rules (Closed Integrity Loop)**

P.3.1 All Pentad interactions SHALL be observational and advisory only.  
P.3.2 The Pentad forms a closed loop:  
- Density → Boundary Stability  
- Boundary Stability → Enforcement Integrity  
- Enforcement Integrity → Reversibility  
- Reversibility → Lineage Continuity  
- Lineage Continuity → Density  

P.3.3 Lineage Continuity acts as the final veto on any recommendation that would break replay determinism.  
P.3.4 All signals between the Pentad and other organs SHALL travel exclusively through the pure information channel via the Equi Interface Bus (EIB).

**P.4 Interaction with Preisach Plane (Appendix K)**

P.4.1 The Pentad (primarily Boundary Stability and Invariant Density) MAY observe aggregate Preisach metrics: active loop count, memory stack depth, wiping frequency, and dominant extrema distribution.

P.4.2 Boundary Stability SHALL veto any proposal that would violate the α < β invariant or allow unauthorized memory modification.

P.4.3 When memory pressure exceeds sealed thresholds, the Pentad MAY recommend (via Pneuma) a strong lawful input excursion designed to trigger controlled wiping-out.

P.4.4 No member of the Pentad, Pneuma, or Θ SHALL write directly to the Preisach plane. Only Class F enforcement actions (through metric-gated inputs) may induce state changes.

P.4.5 All Pentad observations of the Preisach plane MUST be reconstructible from the BLVDB event log.

**P.5 Interaction with Pneuma (Class E) and Class F**

P.5.1 The Pentad feeds the integrity vector \( I(t) \) to Pneuma for evaluation.  
P.5.2 Pneuma produces interpretive signals and risk assessments that the Pentad may use to refine recommendations.  
P.5.3 Class F is the sole organ authorized to generate enforcement actions. The Pentad and Pneuma MAY only provide advisory signals through sealed thresholds.  
P.5.4 Enforcement Integrity SHALL maintain the firewall preventing interpretive layers from influencing enforcement logic except via sealed metric gates.

**P.6 Subordination and Authority Boundaries**

P.6.1 The entire Invariarch Stratum is strictly non-authoritative.  
P.6.2 No Invariarch MAY override ψ₅, SE, or any sealed metric.  
P.6.3 Any Invariarch that attempts to exert direct control SHALL trigger immediate conservative mode and lineage review.

**Appendix H – Constitutional Families and Operator Registry**  
**Version:** v1.0  
**Status:** Canonical, Sealed under CUAP and BEP  
**Cross-references:** Article XI (Operator Loading), Article XXVI (Class D Operator Charter), Article VI (Invariants)

**H.1 Purpose**  
This appendix defines the canonical classification of all operators into doctrinal families and establishes the sealed Operator Registry.

**H.2 Constitutional Families**

The following families are sealed:

- Family I–V: Foundational Pillars (BETA, VU, IOTA, PHI, PSI₅)  
- Family VI–X: Geometric & Hysteresis (Preisach, Curvature, Envelope)  
- Family XI–XV: Execution & Enforcement (HGE, Class F, Execution Envelope)  
- Family XVI–XX: Integrity & Evaluation (Pneuma, Invariarch Pentad, BIV)  
- Family XXI–XXV: Lineage, Mitosis, and Reproduction  
- Family XXVI–XXX: Quantum, Interpretive, and Societal Layers

**H.3 Operator Registry Rules**

H.3.1 All operators SHALL be registered with a unique kebab-case ID, family assignment, and NatSpec++ declaration.  
H.3.2 The Operator Registry is sealed and immutable except through BEP.  
H.3.3 Operators SHALL declare their required invariants and affected domains.  
H.3.4 Class D operators SHALL remain strictly non-authoritative.

**H.4 Sealing Clause**  
The Constitutional Families and Operator Registry defined herein are immutable except through full BEP ratification.
